    We present an adaptive finite element method for the compressible Euler equations, based on a posteriori error estimation of a quantity of interest in terms of a dual problem for the linearized equations. Continuous piecewise linear approximation is used in space and time, with componentwise weighted least-squares stabilization of convection terms and residual-based shock-capturing.
